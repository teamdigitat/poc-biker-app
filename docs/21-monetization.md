## 4. Advertising Model

- **Formats:** native sponsored feed posts, banner placements in Marketplace/Directory screens, sponsored events, brand page promotion.
- **Targeting:** by city, riding-style interest tags, bike ownership (manufacturer/model), rider tier (e.g., target Premium+ users for premium gear ads).
- **Pricing:** CPM for awareness campaigns, CPC for marketplace-intent campaigns; minimum campaign spend thresholds set to maintain ad-quality bar and avoid feed clutter.
- **Guardrail:** ads capped at a max frequency per user session (e.g., max 1 sponsored post per 10 feed items) to protect community trust — a core differentiator vs. ad-heavy competitors.

## 5. Affiliate & Partnership Revenue

- **Insurance:** referral commission for policy comparisons/purchases initiated through the Insurance Hub.
- **Travel/Hotel booking:** commission from booking partners for hotel/camping reservations made via route-planning stop suggestions.
- **Gear affiliate links:** creator/influencer affiliate links tracked and commissioned (also feeds Creator Economy revenue share — see below).

## 6. Creator Economy

- Verified Creators earn a share of: affiliate link commissions, sponsored content facilitated through the platform, and a revenue share on paid content/courses they publish (Phase 3).
- Riding Verse takes a platform fee (target: 15–20%) on creator-facilitated brand deals booked through the in-app Brand Marketplace (Phase 3+).

## 7. Training & Certification Revenue

- Riding schools/academies list paid courses; Riding Verse takes a platform fee (target: 15%) on course enrollments booked in-app.
- Certification issuance fee (small flat fee) for digitally verified certificates.

## 8. Roadside Assistance & Services

- Commission/referral fee from verified roadside assistance partner network per completed service request.
- Potential future flat-fee "Roadside Assistance Plan" add-on (insurance-like annual plan) — Future Vision, requires partner underwriting exploration.

## 9. Pricing Philosophy

- **Safety features are never paywalled** — SOS, crash detection, and basic emergency contacts remain free on all tiers permanently. This is a deliberate trust-building and growth decision, not just an ethical one: free safety features drive viral, family-led adoption that no paywall could replicate.
- Monetization focuses on **convenience, scale, and commerce** (more storage, more AI credits, marketplace transactions, events) rather than gating safety.

## 10. Monetization Roadmap by Phase

- **MVP:** Subscriptions only (Free/Premium), basic Razorpay/IAP integration.
- **Phase 2:** Marketplace commission, event ticketing fees, advertising v1.
- **Phase 3:** Affiliate programs, training revenue share, creator economy tools, API licensing groundwork.
- **Future:** Bike rental/travel booking commission, white-label licensing, insurance usage-based pricing partnerships.
