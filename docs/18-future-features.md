### Key Missing Opportunities Across Competitors

1. No competitor combines **crash detection + community + marketplace + motorsport pathway** in one product.
2. Most safety apps (Life360, Liberty Rider) are car/general-purpose or foreign-market-first, not tuned to Indian road conditions and rider behavior.
3. No credible **digital motorsport career pathway** exists for India — Riding Verse can own this category outright.
4. Marketplace trust layers (verified sellers tied to real ride/community reputation) are unexplored by generic marketplaces (OLX/Facebook Marketplace).
5. OEM apps are single-brand silos; no cross-brand community exists at scale in India.

### Guiding Principle for Future Features

Every future feature must pass three tests before being greenlit for real roadmap inclusion (vs. discussion-only status):

1. **Safety/trust alignment** — does it strengthen or dilute the core safety trust proposition?
2. **India-first viability** — is it viable given India's price sensitivity, network conditions, and regulatory environment?
3. **Defensibility** — does it deepen data/network-effect moats (community, safety data, marketplace trust) rather than just chasing trends?
